Book It Right India Partnership Proposal

USA & India Accounting Collaboration Model

🚀 Objective

The objective of this proposal is to build a practical and scalable accounting outsourcing system by combining the strengths of Book It Right USA and Book It Right India Ltd. The focus is to create a structured approach where client acquisition, execution, and delivery are aligned efficiently.

🎯 Vision

Our long-term vision is to build a scalable global accounting firm based on trust, consistency, and strong execution. The goal is to develop a sustainable partnership model that grows steadily while maintaining quality and long-term client relationships.

Built with Trust • Clarity • Growth

🤝 Partnership Structure

Book It Right India Ltd will operate as a joint partnership between both partners with a 50% – 50% net profit sharing model. This structure is designed to ensure fairness, transparency, and aligned long-term incentives for both sides.

🇺🇸 Book It Right USA

This entity will focus on client-facing responsibilities and market presence.

  • Client acquisition and onboarding
  • Handling communication with clients
  • Pricing decisions based on market conditions
  • Maintaining and strengthening client relationships

🇮🇳 Book It Right India Ltd

This entity will focus on execution, delivery, and operational efficiency.

  • Execution of accounting and bookkeeping work
  • Hiring and managing the India-based team
  • Performing quality checks and final review
  • Developing SOPs and ensuring process consistency

⚙️ Workflow

The workflow is designed to ensure smooth coordination between client handling and execution teams.

💼 Services

The focus will be on providing comprehensive accounting support services to clients.

  • End-to-End Accounting
  • Bookkeeping and Reconciliation
  • Management Reporting and Analysis
  • Accounts Payable and Receivable
  • Payroll Processing
  • Compliance Management

💰 Pricing Model

A base transfer price of $40/hour will be maintained between India and USA entities. Client pricing will remain flexible based on client profile, workload, and market conditions, allowing Book It Right USA to optimize value.

📊 Engagement Model

Each client engagement is expected to start at approximately 7 hours per week, with flexibility depending on the scope and complexity of work.

🕒 Working Coordination

A minimum overlap of 3 hours during morning (EST) will be maintained between India and USA teams to ensure communication efficiency. This can be adjusted based on specific client requirements.

🧾 Billing & Payroll

The billing structure is designed to maintain clarity and transparency between both entities.

  • Book It Right USA will bill end clients on a weekly basis
  • Book It Right India Ltd will bill Book It Right USA on a bi-weekly basis
  • Weekly timesheets will be shared for tracking and transparency
  • Employee payroll in India will be processed on a bi-weekly basis (every 15 days)
